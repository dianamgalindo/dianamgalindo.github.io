About

I am Diana, a digital product designer based in London-UK, specialising in web design, UX | UI, front-end development, and project management.

I hold a Creative Computing degree from the University of Arts London, where my work gravitated toward sustainability practices, ethical use of technology and data, digital green skills, and the application of technology to address current environmental issues. I am passionate about technology that has an impact, and I enjoy exploring distinct possible outcomes by making use of Design Fiction, as a thinking strategy.

Currently, I have an interest in growing my design experience and practice through a UX designer or Digital Product Designer role.

Interested in: Generative AI | Hackathons | Gallery Exhibitions| Emergent technologies | Languages and Outdoor Activities.
